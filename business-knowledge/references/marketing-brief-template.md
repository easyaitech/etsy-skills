## Rules

- Default validity: 7-14 days.
- Use at most 5 Knowledge Cards.
- Do not override `BRAND_MARKETING.md` or `MARKETING_PLATFORM.md`.
- If marketing/platform strategy files are missing, say so in `Strategy base status`.
- Include English buyer-facing copy examples when useful; keep strategy explanation in Chinese.
