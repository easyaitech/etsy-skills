# Weekly Knowledge Notes Template

路径：

```text
<workspace>/knowledge/weekly/YYYY-WW.md
```

模板：

```markdown
# Weekly Knowledge Notes: YYYY-WW

Generated: YYYY-MM-DD
Sources folder: ../raw/YYYY-WW/

## Executive Summary
- 3-5 bullets only.

## Signals

### Signal: {short name}
- Evidence:
  - Source:
  - Observed detail:
- Why it might matter:
- Possible business use:
- Caveat:
- Suggested status: active / watch

## Not Useful This Week

### Rejected Signal: {short name}
- Source:
- Why rejected:
- Do not use for:

## Candidate Cards

### Card 1: {title}
- 一句话摘要:
- 来源:
- 适用场景:
- 关键词标签:
- 建议动作:
- 有效状态:
- 边界:

## Wiki Updates Proposed
- {topic-slug}.md: append / merge summary.
```

## Rules

- Keep weekly notes concise; raw detail belongs in `raw/YYYY-WW/`.
- Do not promote every signal into a card.
- Prefer `watch` when evidence is weak or seasonal.
- Keep rejected signals. They prevent the same bad idea from resurfacing next week.
